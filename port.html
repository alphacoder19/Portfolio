Student Result Management System

The Student Result Management System is a comprehensive web-based application designed to manage and display students' academic results efficiently. This system is aimed at schools, colleges, and universities to streamline the process of recording, calculating, and distributing student grades.

Admin Login: Secure login for administrators to manage student records and results. Student Login: Secure login for students to view their individual results.

Weather Forecasting App

The Weather Forecasting App is a user-friendly application that provides accurate and real-time weather information for any location in the world. The app is designed to help users plan their day and week by providing detailed weather forecasts, current conditions, and weather alerts.

Current Weather Conditions: Displays current temperature, humidity, wind speed, and weather conditions (e.g., sunny, rainy, cloudy) for the selected location. 7-Day Forecast: Provides a detailed weather forecast for the next seven days, including high and low temperatures, precipitation chances, and weather conditions. Hourly Forecast: Offers an hour-by-hour breakdown of weather conditions for the next 24 hours. Weather Alerts: Notifies users of severe weather conditions, such as storms, heavy rain, or heatwaves, in their area.
